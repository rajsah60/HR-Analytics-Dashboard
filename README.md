# HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition and identify key factors contributing to workforce turnover. It provides HR teams and business leaders with actionable insights into employee demographics, salary, job roles, education, age groups, and years at the company.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Identify departments and job roles with the highest attrition.
- Understand the impact of age, salary, education, and gender on employee turnover.
- Support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 👥 Total Employees: **1,470**
- 🚪 Attrition Count: **237**
- 📉 Attrition Rate: **16.1%**
- 🎂 Average Age: **36.92 Years**
- 💰 Average Income: **6.5K**
- 📅 Average Years at Company: **7.01**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

- **Attrition by Gender** – Compare employee attrition between male and female employees.
- **Attrition by Education** – Analyze attrition across different educational backgrounds.
- **Attrition by Age Group** – Identify which age groups experience the highest turnover.
- **Attrition by Salary Slab** – Understand the relationship between salary ranges and attrition.
- **Attrition by Job Role** – Discover job roles with the highest employee turnover.
- **Attrition by Years at Company** – Analyze how tenure affects employee attrition.
- **Department Filter** – View insights for Human Resources, Research & Development, and Sales.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- The overall employee attrition rate is **16.1%**.
- Employees earning **up to 5K** salary have the highest attrition.
- The **26–35** age group shows the highest employee turnover.
- **Laboratory Technicians**, **Sales Executives**, and **Research Scientists** experience the highest attrition.
- Employees with **Life Sciences** and **Medical** educational backgrounds contribute significantly to overall attrition.
- Most employees leave within the first few years of joining the company.

---

## 📈 Business Value

This dashboard enables HR professionals to:

- Reduce employee turnover
- Identify high-risk employee groups
- Improve retention strategies
- Support workforce planning
- Make data-driven HR decisions
